4-Write a short note on Tag and element with an example?

Answer- Tags are the starting and ending parts of an HTML element. They begin with < symbol and end with > symbol.
Example- 
 <h1> - in < > is known as tag.
An HTML element is defined by a starting tag( <>). If the element contains other content, it ends with a closing tag( </>). 
Example-
<h1>HTML defines the content and structure of
your website</h1>-
This is called an element. <h1> is starting tag,</h1> is closing tag
