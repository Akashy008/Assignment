4-Write a short note on Tag and element with an example?

Answer-Tag- Markup tags are usually called as HTML tag. They begin with opeining tag and end with closing tag.
Example- 
 <h1></h1> - is known as opening tag, closing tag .
Element- An HTML element is defined by a starting tag( <>), some content in middle, it ends with a closing tag( </>). 
Example-
<h1>content</h1>-
This is called an element. <h1> is starting tag,</h1> is closing tag
